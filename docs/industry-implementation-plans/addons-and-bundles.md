# Addons and Bundles

## Cross-Pack Addons
- **Advanced Analytics**: cohort/pivot workspace, scheduled reports, anomaly alerts, export to BI.
- **Integrations**: payment gateways, SMS/email providers, accounting export bridges, government/fiscal devices where required.
- **Identity & Access**: SSO/SAML/OIDC, audit trails, approval workflows, delegated admin.
- **Document Automation**: templated PDFs (invoices, delivery notes, report cards, certificates), e-signature hooks.
- **Data Protect & Continuity**: backup/export pack, data retention policies, legal holds.

## School Addons
- **Learning module**: assignments, content delivery, LMS-grade sync.
- **Transport**: routes/stops, ridership billing, vehicle inspections.
- **Meal plans**: cafeteria menus, prepaid balances, subsidy rules.
- **PTA/Events**: event scheduling, ticketing, volunteer signups, fundraising.

## Auto Addons
- **Marketing automation**: campaign journeys, lead scoring, nurture emails/SMS.
- **Insurance & financing connectors**: rate shopping, eligibility checks, lender document kits.
- **Inventory syndication**: push listings to marketplaces/website with price updates.
- **Service bay management**: technician scheduling, parts reservations, RO billing.

## Retail Addons
- **Loyalty**: points, tiers, rewards catalog, burn/earn rules, member pricing.
- **Ecommerce connector**: sync catalog/orders/customers with web storefronts and marketplaces.
- **Procurement automation**: supplier scorecards, suggested POs, contract pricing.
- **Workforce**: rota/scheduling, time clock, performance KPIs at store/associate level.

## Bundles to Sell
- **School Growth Bundle**: core school pack + learning module + transport + analytics.
- **Auto Revenue Bundle**: auto pack + marketing automation + insurance/financing connectors + inventory syndication.
- **Retail Omnichannel Bundle**: retail pack + loyalty + ecommerce connector + procurement automation.
- **Finance Integrity Bundle**: any pack + posting-engine housekeeping, reconciliation, and backup/export add-ons.
